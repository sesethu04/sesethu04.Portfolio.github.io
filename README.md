# Reflection on Coding My CV in Markdown Using the STAR Method

## 🟡 Situation (Context and Understanding of the Task)
As part of the portfolio development assignment at CPUT, we were required to design a professional digital portfolio and include our curriculum vitae (CV) using Markdown language. This task aimed to assess our ability to apply industry-relevant tools and demonstrate an understanding of modern documentation practices used in the Information Technology field.

---

## 🔵 Task (Application of Knowledge and Skills)
The task required me to transform my CV from a traditional format into a Markdown document while maintaining a professional structure and clear presentation of information. This involved applying my knowledge of Markdown syntax and ensuring that key sections such as personal details, education, skills, and project experience were logically organized and easy to interpret. The CV also needed to be suitable for integration into an online portfolio environment.

---

## 🟢 Action (Implementation and Problem-Solving)
To address the task requirements, I applied appropriate Markdown elements such as headings, subheadings, and bullet points to structure the document effectively. I demonstrated attention to detail by ensuring consistency in formatting and alignment throughout the CV. Where challenges arose, such as maintaining readability without advanced styling, I adapted by simplifying content and focusing on clarity. I also evaluated my work by previewing the document across Markdown-supported platforms to ensure correct rendering and usability.

---

## 🔴 Result (Reflection and Learning)
The final product met the task requirements by presenting a clear, well-structured, and professionally formatted CV within a digital portfolio. This activity enhanced my understanding of Markdown as a practical tool for technical documentation and strengthened my ability to apply theoretical knowledge in a practical context. Furthermore, it developed my problem-solving skills and attention to detail. The experience has prepared me to create effective digital documentation and reinforced the importance of using appropriate tools in professional IT environments.
